2. Short Description / Purpose

The OLA Analytics Dashboard is a comprehensive data visualization project built to monitor and evaluate ride-hailing business performance. It helps stakeholders understand booking trends, revenue distribution, cancellation behavior, and service quality across different vehicle categories.

This dashboard is useful for business analysts, operations teams, and product managers who want data-driven insights into mobility platforms like OLA or Uber.

4. Data Source

Source: Synthetic business dataset modeled on real-world ride-hailing operations (inspired by OLA and Uber).

The dataset captures complete ride transactions, including booking and driver IDs, ride status, vehicle types, booking value, trip distance, payment methods, customer and driver ratings, and detailed cancellation reasons.

It is structured to closely resemble real operational data used by mobility companies, making it suitable for practical business analysis and KPI-driven insights.
